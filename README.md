# ✨ Spark

**Elegant Digital Love Letters for Your Special One**

Spark is a premium, beautifully crafted web experience that lets you create heartfelt, aesthetic, and emotionally resonant digital love pages for your crush, partner, or anyone who holds a special place in your heart.

With custom romantic typography, graceful animations, soft gradients, and memorable clean URLs, Spark turns your feelings into unforgettable shareable moments — perfect for confessions, anniversaries, birthdays, Valentine’s Day, or simply saying “I’m thinking of you.”

**No coding. No sign-up. Pure elegance.** ❤️

## 🚀 Live Demo

**[Create Your Spark →](https://zidhuxd.com/spark)**

## ✨ Key Features

- **Instant Page Generator** — Create a stunning love page in seconds with just two names
- **Premium Romantic Typography** — Hand-selected custom fonts (`Antigritas` & `Provicali`)
- **Sophisticated Animations** — Subtle sparkles, soft gradients, and heart-fluttering micro-interactions
- **Clean & Memorable URLs** — Beautiful links like `/spark/priyalovesarjun`
- **Fully Responsive Design** — Pixel-perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Lightning-Fast** — Pure static HTML, CSS & JavaScript (no backend)
- **Bonus Experience** — Includes a playful surprise page

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- HTML5, CSS3 & Vanilla JavaScript
- Custom romantic fonts
- Modern elegant animations
- Vercel (with clean URL routing via `vercel.json`)

## 💌 How to Use

1. Visit the live demo
2. Enter your name and your special someone’s name
3. Click “Create your Spark” ✨
4. Copy the unique link and share the magic 💌
